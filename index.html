About Us

Advanced Imaging and Minimally Invasive Techniques for You and Your Family's Health

Dr. Sanket Kulkarni is a dedicated Consultant Interventional Radiologist at Yashoda Hospitals, Malakpet, with advanced training from esteemed institutions like SGPGIMS, Lucknow. Known for his precision and passion, he has contributed significantly to the field through impactful research, peer-reviewed publications, and engaging presentations at prominent conferences and he specializes in:

  • Neuro Interventions: Tumor Ablation, Chemoembolization, and Radioembolization
  • GI and Hepatic Interventions: Peripheral Artery Disease Treatment, Varicose Vein Ablation, and Embolization for GI Bleeding
  • Vascular Interventions: Liver Transplant Support and Biliary Drainage Procedures
